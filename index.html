Simulation- VR Flash Flood Simulation

We created a pseudo simulation of a flash flood and created a VR based world which would be used to train people in a disaster.
A recovery solution was created for Florida International University as a collaborative project.

Simulation -VR Brain Signal Translation

This was an ambitious project where we used the EEG signals from the brain and visual stimulation through VR to create a model that could understand basic actions and words.
This would enable us to communicate with people with various ailments where direct communication is not possible. This would enable digital communications just through thought.

Machine Learning Brain Segmentation

We built this project as part of my final year assignment in college. We trained an ML based model to help in brain segmentation using 3D MRI data.
This would help in identifying tumours and other malignant and non-malignant entities, using 3D data rather than slices used by doctors.
This helps in increasing the accuracy with which these tumours can be detected.

Neural Networks Baby Cry Translator and Circadian Rhythm Inducer

New parents always find it difficult to understand what their babies try to tell them.
Using a clinical dataset with classified baby cries we were able to train a model that would act as a baby cry translator.
The other issues that parents face is the odd timings of their babys sleep patterns, this is primarily due to lack of a circadian rhythm or human body clock. Hence, the idea was to create an LSTM model that will generate lullabies which will induce the formation of this rhythm and help put the children to sleep.

Simulation - VRADHD Research

We created a VR world and used the tracking from the headset to detect ADHD symptoms and also help increase focus through therapy.

Machine LearningCIE-Score Management System

In my college we have a system of internal grading for assignments and other intermediate tests as part of the CCE program. There are multiple hand graded tests and reports that need to be generated as part of a project. We created a system that uses a Machine learning Model that forms the OCR technology to automatically pick up the grades from a hand written format and was extended to attendance and other manual processes as well. With all the data the system ingested, we were also able to build a dashboard that would help teachers understand the analytics of their class.

CloudRemote Print Tools

I am one of the co-founder of a Start-up that was formed during the height of the pandemic.
Due to the requirement of hard copy printouts by the university and the fact that students were now spread all across the country, logistics and safety of the students were the main concerns.
So we created a system that enables students to upload their projects and through the power of the cloud were able to remotely print it near the University for submission. We enabled the printing of 50,000 + pages remotely and helped students remain safe during the pandemic and also helped the local printing businesses around the university keep their operations running.
